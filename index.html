SERVICES WE PROVIDE

We provide efficient end to end Waste Management Solutions for all types of waste so that your waste doesn't remain a burden for "Mother Earth" and yourself anymore.

Enabling Segregation at Source

We help in enabling Segregation at source for various communities so that their waste can be treated and processed further efficiently and the least amount reaches landfills.
-

Residential Complexes & Societies

Post enabling segration at source, we setup composting units so as to treat wet waste at source and reducing the waste going out by more than 60%. Remaining dry waste is then sorted and sent for upcycling and recycling.

Corporate Offices

We handle all types of E-waste and Dry waste (Eg. Computers, papers, files etc) for corporate offices. We ensure data destruction before processing so that your data remains confidential. -

Institutions

For All Govt. and Private Institutions (Schools, Colleges etc) we provide complete solutions for E-Waste and dry waste which is generated in bulk quantity in such institutions. Wet waste treatement facility is also available.
-

Restaurants

For restaurants waste we provide solution to all types of food(wet) and packaging(dry) wastes so that this waste can be minimized and treated at source itself.
-

Complete Waste Solution & Support

With complete end to end waste management solutions we ensure you that your waste is no more a burden to "Mother Earth". Also we provide active support for our collaborators and ensure the the process is being followed correctly.
